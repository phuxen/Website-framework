Paul Huxen

Graphic designer & beard owner

Hello there, I'm a bit of a designer. Always have been ever since I broke out a box of crayons and drew all over the walls. Thankfully now I don't draw on walls, I design for fashion labels or throw out a typeface through my font foundry – Kraken.

I'll be found creating a UI for a webmail service using HTML5, CSS3 and {LESS}, or knocking up some advertising and web banners in the Adobe CS5 suite.

Bollard

Bollard is a fat and cuddly sans serif typeface which was created during typographic experiments with simple shapes and elements. I had seen many fat fonts however these were still all quite angular and didn't have the softer appearance that I craved. So I took it upon myself to construct this font made of circles and gentle curved corners and unleashed it into the wild a few months later.

Let The Kids Dance

Let The Kids Dance are a fashion company that take new graduates and talent from the worlds of design, art and fashion. My designs were to be used as part of the first women's clothing collection. The designs include previous work such as the typefaces Retro Disco and Fat Marker along with a new layout design for playing cards. New artwork was created especially for the collection such as the hand drawn slogan "…and so we cannot know ourselves…" and the Paisley Monster.
